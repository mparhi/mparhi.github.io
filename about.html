Megha Parhi – About

Short Bio

I recently graduated with my M.S. in Electrical Engineering from The University of Texas at Austin (UT) under the supervision of Professor Ahmed H. Tewfik. While at UT, I worked on classifying imaginary vowels from EEG Brain-Computer Interface signals using Deep Learning models.

Prior to joining graduate school I worked at a medical device company. And even before that, I received my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from the University of Minnesota – Twin Cities in May 2015.
